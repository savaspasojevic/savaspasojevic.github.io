A Brief History of Efforts and Initiatives.

Current Affairs

It is without further adieu that I announce the commencement of the third edition of my website. This edition is the third attempt at devising a website to serve as a professional outlet for my self-directed mathematical and literary researches. I must emphasize here that what I term 'research' is a collection of investigations into certain subjects of interest, namely those of probability, analysis, and economics. Given the unique circumstances in which the world finds itself, it is not a dull and uninteresting task to monitor current events. Aside from reading and writing, I have spent considerable time analyzing the state of the world and making connections between my research and the current trends. This has resulted in the attainment of as complete of an understanding of mathematics, markets, economics, and history that I can possibly attain on my own.

Decisions

My researches started with the problem of modeling sequences of events in an effort to price derivative securities, so that I may construct sound trading strategies. I created my website in 2017 anticipating I would use it in the future. The first edition of my website, in late 2019, featured simple options pricing formulas for European calls and puts. The second edition of my website in May 2020 was an attempt at education, something that I had appropriately dubbed "Project Free Exploration." I was reluctant to go through with the idea since my knowledge of foundational mathematics was not yet complete. It was only in the first week of September after an arduous month of mental recovery that I meditated for several days in a row and finally gained as complete of an understanding of the big picture as possible. This was achieved by considering difficult philosophical dilemmas, which had previously been posed only in a hypothetical sense, but have recently culminated in very real exersices in decision making.

Final Showcase

The final and third edition of my website will serve as a showcase of some of that which I've worked on, as well as a useful timeline to help paint a picture of what it was that had been done behind closed doors. On the mathematics tab you will find the schedule for my lecutures and montages. I have thought a great deal of that which I will release to the world, and even though I may not be able to translate my thoughts directly into the magnificent motion pictures or books that I would want them to be, they have been constructed for you guys. For entertaintment, enjoyment, freedom, and as thanks for your patience and support.
